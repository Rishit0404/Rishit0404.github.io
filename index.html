Hey there! I'm Rishit Mittal, a first-year B.Tech student fuelled by an obsession with Data Science. I believe every dataset tells a story — and I want to be the one who reads it.

My coding journey began with C Programming, where I learned the fundamentals of logic and problem-solving.
